The Right Material

A branching scenario for new SAP users learning the purchase-to-pay business process. A junior employee must create a purchase order — but two nearly identical material entries lead to very different business outcomes. Three decision paths, each with authentic consequences and a targeted debrief.

Design Rationale

New SAP users face a real risk: two materials with nearly identical names can have completely different downstream consequences. The scenario is designed around a false relief moment — the wrong choice confirms successfully before the error surfaces — because that's how real procurement mistakes actually unfold. A second branch teaches not just whether to ask for help, but who holds the right knowledge in a time-pressured situation. Both design choices serve the same business goal: building the habit of verification before submission.

Soft Skills Jeopardy

A Jeopardy-style game built in Articulate Storyline for HUB International, covering three soft skills themes: stakeholder communication, conflict resolution, and building trust and credibility. Designed for a mixed audience — from new employees to senior leaders — where a shared understanding of these behaviours directly impacts team performance and client relationships.

Design Rationale

Soft skills training has a retention problem: passive content gets forgotten fast. The game format was chosen to drive genuine retrieval through scoring and competition — creating engagement that a standard knowledge check never would. Covering all three themes ensures every participant — regardless of role or seniority — builds a common foundation across stakeholder communication, conflict resolution, and trust and credibility.
